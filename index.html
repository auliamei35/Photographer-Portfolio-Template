About Me

Visual storyteller seeking the extraordinary in the mundane.

I am Alexander, a photographer based in New York City with over a decade of experience in editorial and portrait photography. My work is driven by a deep curiosity about the human condition and the environments we inhabit.

Featured in Vogue, Kinfolk, and Cereal Magazine, I specialize in creating imagery that feels timeless yet distinctly modern. I believe that every frame should evoke a feeling, not just capture a scene.
